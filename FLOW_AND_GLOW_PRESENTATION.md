# Flow and Glow - Presentation Outline

## 8-Slide Presentation Structure

---

## **Slide 1: The Modern Life Challenge**

### Title: "Life in the Modern City"

### Content:
- **The urban lifestyle has distanced us from nature and drawn us closer to materialism**
- This disconnect has clear negative impacts on our souls, bodies, and minds
- We are living far from nature and closer to material things, and its negative effects are evident on our spirits, bodies, and minds
- Despite the rapid pace of modern life, we're experiencing:
  - Increased stress and anxiety
  - Physical health challenges
  - Mental exhaustion
  - Spiritual emptiness

### Visual Suggestion:
- Split image showing busy city life vs. peaceful natural wellness activities
- Icons representing stress, disconnection, and imbalance

---

## **Slide 2: The Need for Balance**

### Title: "Technology & Soul Food: Finding Balance"

### Content:
- **While we celebrate scientific and technological progress**, we must acknowledge what we're losing
- The revolution between science and technology has brought incredible benefits:
  - Advanced healthcare
  - Global connectivity
  - Improved living standards
- **But we need more than just technological advancement**
- We need:
  - **Spiritual nourishment**
  - **Physical health**
  - **Mental wellness**
- That's why we created **Flow and Glow** — to create balanced channels for holistic well-being

### Visual Suggestion:
- Balance scale showing technology on one side and wellness (yoga, meditation, fitness) on the other
- Icons for spiritual, physical, and mental health

---

## **Slide 3: What is Flow and Glow?**

### Title: "What is the Flow and Glow App?"

### Content:
- **Flow and Glow is a wellness marketplace platform** that connects people with holistic health and wellness centers
- The app helps users discover and access:
  - Yoga and meditation programs
  - Fitness and movement classes
  - Spiritual wellness sessions
  - Mental health workshops
  - Holistic healing services
- **Key Features:**
  - Browse wellness programs from certified centers
  - Compare packages and prices in one place
  - Book and manage subscriptions easily
  - Track your wellness journey
- **Our Mission:** Make holistic wellness accessible to everyone seeking balance in modern life

### Visual Suggestion:
- App mockup showing the main interface
- Isometric illustrations of people doing yoga, meditation, fitness
- Icons representing different wellness categories

---

## **Slide 4: How We Planned the App?**

### Title: "Planning Flow and Glow"

### Content:
- **We started by identifying the problem:**
  - People struggle to find quality wellness centers
  - Information about programs is scattered and hard to compare
  - No centralized platform for booking wellness services
  - Difficult to manage multiple subscriptions

- **Our Solution Approach:**
  - Create a centralized marketplace for wellness services
  - Enable easy comparison of programs and prices
  - Simplify the booking and subscription process
  - Build a community around holistic wellness

- **Planning Process:**
  - Researched user needs and pain points
  - Studied existing wellness platforms
  - Designed a three-tier system (Super Admin, Center Admin, Customer)
  - Mapped out user journeys for each role

### Visual Suggestion:
- Flowchart or mind map showing the planning process
- User persona illustrations
- Problem-solution diagram

## **Slide 5: Why We Chose to Build a Mobile App?**

### Title: "Why Mobile App Over Website?"

### Content:
- People check wellness schedules on-the-go and need quick access to their bookings
- Mobile apps provide better user experience with push notifications for class reminders
- Native features like GPS help users find nearby centers easily
- Mobile apps offer better engagement and personalization for wellness journeys

### Visual Suggestion:
- Mobile phone mockup vs. desktop website
- Icons showing mobile features (GPS, notifications, camera)

---

## **Slide 6: Choosing the Development Platform**

### Title: "Choosing Flutter and Planning the Work"

### Content:
- We initially considered building native apps separately for Android and iOS, but realized cross-platform development would be more efficient
- After researching cross-platform options, we chose Flutter because it has the highest percentage of apps on the App Store
- Flutter has a strong community with excellent documentation and learning resources
- The framework offers powerful features and beautiful UI capabilities that suited our wellness app needs

### Visual Suggestion:
- Flutter logo and comparison with other frameworks
- Tech stack icons (Flutter, Firebase, Figma)
- Development workflow diagram

## **Slide 7: App Flow - Super Admin & Center Admin**

### Title: "The Platform Foundation"

### Content:

**Super Admin Scenario:**
- The app starts with the Super Admin setting up initial configurations and adding wellness categories to the home page
- Super Admin reviews and approves center registration requests from wellness centers wanting to join the platform
- They monitor all platform activities to ensure quality and compliance
- Super Admin provides support to both center admins and customers when needed
- They maintain the overall health and integrity of the Flow and Glow ecosystem

**Center Admin Scenario:**
- Center Admin registers their wellness center and creates a detailed profile with images and descriptions
- They add their wellness programs with pricing, schedules, and package options
- Center Admin monitors customer subscriptions and manages transactions
- They track program performance and customer engagement
- Center Admin provides direct support to their subscribed customers

### Visual Suggestion:
- Split screen showing Super Admin and Center Admin dashboards
- Flow diagram showing approval process
- Icons representing key admin functions

---

## **Slide 8: App Functionality - Customer Journey**

### Title: "The Customer Experience"

### Content:

**Customer Scenario:**
- Customer opens the app and browses wellness centers by categories like Yoga, Meditation, and Fitness
- They find a suitable program that matches their wellness goals and schedule preferences
- Customer subscribes to the program and completes the payment securely through the app
- They can view all their subscribed programs in a personalized calendar view
- Customer receives notifications for upcoming sessions and can track their wellness journey progress

## **Presentation Notes:**

### **Design Principles:**
- Use calming colors (blues, greens, purples) that reflect wellness
- Include nature-inspired imagery
- Show actual app screenshots
- Use isometric illustrations for concepts
- Keep text concise with bullet points

### **Visual Assets Needed:**
1. Modern city vs. nature imagery
2. Balance/wellness icons
3. App mockups and screenshots
4. Technology icons (Flutter, Firebase, etc.)
5. User journey flowcharts

2. **Solution** (Slide 3): Introduce Flow and Glow
3. **Process** (Slides 4-6): Show how we built it
4. **Product** (Slides 7-8): Demonstrate the result

- Three-tier system serves all stakeholders
